This game is an excerpted part of an intended larger tech-demo.

In terms of gameplay, it involves six-degrees-of-freedom space -flight and -combat.

To see the controls for the game while playing, press the F1 key.

If the key is simply tapped, then the help will be displayed until it is tapped again. If the key is held, then the help will be displayed until it is released.

To pause the game while playing, thus bringing up a pause-menu, press the "escape" key.
